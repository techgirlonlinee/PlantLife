mission

Like you, we are devastated by the acceralatered effects of climate change, rapid rate of deforestation, and decline of animals species. We know you want to make a change. Us too. But we also know that caring people can have busy lives. Don't worry though, our social enterprise got you covered.

Plant Life offers nine plant species, each provides different and much needed benefits to the ecosystem. Purchase as many of each species as you like. Then you can sit back and relax - you have done your part. We will go on the grounds to do all the tree planting and nurturing for you. All our benefits goes to support the struggling local farmers & their communities as well as grass-root activism to stop deforestation.
